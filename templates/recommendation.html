RECOMMENDATION

Accommodation and Transportation



Accommodation and transportation are essential for all international students in Melbourne. Average rental price for 1-bedroom apartment is $300 pw in Melbourne city, however the rent will be much cheaper if you choose to live in the suburb which is around $200PW.

Website: www.realestate.com.au.

Public transport in Melbourne is cheaper than a taxi or uber. More price can be viewed on the PTV website.

Website: www.ptv.vic.gov.au.

Eating and Drinking in Melbourne



Melbourne is a city with full of delicious food and drinks. The average cost per person is about $40. There are serval apps and websites you can search good restaurants and bars, You also can see the menu and average cost per person there.

Website: www.zomato.com.

Events and Activities



There are many activities in Melbourne every week, you can join different communities and activities in your leisure time. Some events and activities are free for international students but Always check the price and fees before you go.


Website: www.whatson.melbourne.vic.gov.au.
